## 摘要
当今行星系统的结构蕴含着其形成和迁移历史的信息。热木星（HJs，轨道周期P ≤ 10天，半径R_p > 8 R_⊕）的起源长期以来一直是一个争论的话题。尽管大多数热木星被发现有“孤独”的特性，但也有少数热木星在内轨道上拥有小型伴星（截至2026年5月已知有8个）。这种独特的结构暗示了温和的盘迁移机制。在本研究中，我们呈现了多行星系统TOI-1533的发现和表征，该系统包含一个内部亚海王星（TOI-1533 b，轨道周期P_orb = 3.63天，半径R_p = 3.15 R_⊕）和一个外部热巨行星（TOI-1533 c，轨道周期P_orb = 8.06天，半径R_p > 7.5 R_⊕），后者含有大量H/He（密度ρ_p < 0.48 g cm^{-3}），两者都绕一颗活跃的K矮星运行（有效温度T_eff ≈ 5130 K；视星等V ≈ 11）。我们对来自径向速度（HARPS-N）和凌星（TESS）的恒星活动和行星信号进行联合建模，使我们能够探测到它们的开普勒信号（大约10σ）并分离出恒星调制。在多维高斯过程框架中包含同步测光是光谱活动指标的基本补充，能够将行星信号从恒星活动中解缠。两个已确认行星的质量比（M_b / M_c约0.8），加上外部大伴星的超级海王星质量（M_c ≈ 40 M_⊕），使得该系统与其他少数拥有低质量内伴星的热木星系统相比显得不同寻常。

## Abstract
The present-day architecture of planetary systems contains information about their formation and migration histories. The origin of hot Jupiters (HJs, P $\lesssim$ 10 d, $R_{\rm p} > 8 R_\oplus$) has long been a matter of debate. While most of them are found to be ``lonely'', there is a rare population of HJs hosting small companions on inner orbits (eight known as of May 2026). Their peculiar architecture suggests a gentle disc-migration mechanism. In this study, we present the discovery and characterisation of the multi-planet system TOI-1533, comprising an inner sub-Neptune (TOI-1533 b, $P_{\rm orb} = 3.63$ d, $R_{\rm p} = 3.15 R_\oplus$) and an outer hot giant planet (TOI-1533 c, $P_{\rm orb} = 8.06$ d, $R_{\rm p} > 7.5 R_\oplus$) with substantial H/He by mass ($ρ_{\rm p} < 0.48$ g cm$^{-3}$), both transiting an active K-dwarf star ($T_{\rm eff} \approx$ 5130 K; $V$ (mag) $\approx$ 11). Our joint modelling of stellar activity and planetary signals from radial velocities (HARPS-N) and transits (TESS) allows us to detect their Keplerian signals (approximately $10~σ$) and to isolate the stellar modulation. The inclusion of simultaneous photometry in the multi-dimensional Gaussian processes formalism was a fundamental addition to the spectroscopic activity indicators, enabling the disentanglement of stellar activity from planetary signals. The mass ratio of the two confirmed planets ($M_{\rm b} / M_{\rm c}$ about 0.8), together with the super-Neptune mass of the large outer companion ($M_{\rm c} \approx 40 M_\oplus$), makes this system unusual compared to the other few HJs with low-mass inner companions.
