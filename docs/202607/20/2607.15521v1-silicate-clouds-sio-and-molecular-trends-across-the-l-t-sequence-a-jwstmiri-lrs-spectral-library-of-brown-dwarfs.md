## 摘要
我们介绍了利用JWST的MIRI LRS进行的巡天项目的结果，获得了23个L0至T6矮星的5-14微米R~100光谱。这一光谱类型范围涵盖了硅酸盐凝聚云的形成、增长和沉降，以及褐矮星大气中甲烷和氨的出现。我们以前所未有的细节研究了L0光谱类型下硅酸盐吸收的初始出现以及该特征随光谱类型的演化。我们首次在矮星中探测到7.5-9.5微米的气态SiO吸收，基于Spitzer IRS和JWST光谱的综合分析，在M5.5至L2.5矮星中均有探测。这一特征以前在K型和M型巨星中被探测到，其吸收强度大于更高重力的M矮星。相反，低重力的L矮星显示出比场重力L矮星更弱的SiO吸收。我们还确认了水、氨和甲烷吸收随光谱类型的变化趋势，这与之前Spitzer IRS观测结果一致。我们报告了可能在一个T3.5矮星中探测到CS2，但需要更高色散光谱来确认。我们发现了两颗新分解的双星，确认了两颗近似等流量的未分解候选双星，并识别出另外两个可能的非等流量双星。在考虑了分解和候选双星的污染后，我们发现单颗场矮星比现有经验关系暗约0.15-0.20星等。所呈现的光谱库为未来解释褐矮星或直接成像或凌星系外行星的JWST MIRI光谱提供了分光光度校准基础。

## Abstract
We present the results of a survey program with JWST's MIRI LRS that obtained 5$-$14 $μ$m R~100 spectra of twenty-three L0$-$T6 dwarfs. This spectral type range spans the formation, growth, and sedimentation of silicate condensate clouds, as well as the appearance of methane and ammonia in the atmospheres of brown dwarfs. The initial onset of silicate absorption at spectral type L0 as well as the evolution of this feature with spectral type are both examined in unprecedented detail. We detect 7.5$-$9.5 $μ$m gas-phase SiO absorption for the first time in dwarf stars, with detections in M5.5$-$L2.5 dwarfs from combined Spitzer IRS and JWST spectra. This feature has previously been detected in K and M giants, with stronger absorption in these giants than in higher-gravity M dwarfs. In contrast, low-gravity L dwarfs show weaker SiO absorption than field-gravity L dwarfs. We also confirm water, ammonia, and methane absorption trends with spectral type previously seen in Spitzer IRS observations. We report the possible detection of CS2 in a T3.5 dwarf, although confirmation would require higher-dispersion spectroscopy. We find two newly resolved binaries, confirm two near-equal flux unresolved candidate binaries, and identify two additional possible non-equal flux binaries. After accounting for contamination by resolved and candidate binaries, we find that single field dwarfs are ~0.15$-$0.20 mag underluminous compared to existing empirical relations. The presented spectral library offers a spectrophotometrically calibrated basis for future interpretation of JWST MIRI spectra of brown dwarfs or of directly imaged or transiting exoplanets.

- **高信噪比与波长覆盖**：相比Spitzer IRS，JWST MIRI LRS的灵敏度提升约50倍，使弱特征（如SiO吸收）的首次探测成为可能。
